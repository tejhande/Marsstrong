About Marsstrong International
Marsstrong International, established in November 2017, is a leading export-import consulting firm and comprehensive business solutions provider based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. As a trusted partner for over 1000+ clients, we specialize in international trade facilitation, company registrations, tax compliance, shipping logistics, and import-export consultation services.

Our expertise spans across multiple business structures including Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP), Partnership Firms, and Proprietorship setups. We excel in customs clearance, LUT Bond processing, and provide specialized DGFT-related services in both Mumbai and Delhi.

Core Competencies: IEC Registration, Advance License processing, MEIS/SEIS schemes, TED Refund assistance, SION Norms Fixation, SCOMET Export Licenses, and comprehensive post-export activities including license verification and bond cancellation.

With our commitment to excellence and client-focused approach, Marsstrong International has become the preferred choice for businesses seeking reliable international trade solutions and regulatory compliance support across India.
