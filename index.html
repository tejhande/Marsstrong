Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to get an IEC code?

We typically process IEC code registrations within 3-5 working days after receiving complete documentation. The DGFT usually issues the IEC code within 72 hours of application submission if all documents are in order.

Can NRIs register companies in India through Marsstrong?

Yes, we specialize in company registration for NRIs and foreign nationals. We handle all requirements including DIN, DSC, name approval, MOA/AOA drafting, and RBI compliance. The process typically takes 10-15 working days.

What is the RODTEP scheme and how can it benefit my business?

The Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RODTEP) scheme provides exporters with refunds of embedded duties and taxes. Benefits range from 0.5% to 4.3% of FOB value depending on the product. We help assess eligibility, calculate benefits, and file claims.

Do you provide support for customs clearance at all Indian ports?

Yes, we have a network of customs brokers at all major ports including Mumbai (JNPT), Chennai, Kolkata, Mundra, and Delhi Air Cargo. We handle documentation, duty payment, examination coordination, and clearance procedures.

What is the cost for your company registration services?

Our pricing starts at ₹8,999 for Private Limited Company registration including government fees, DSC, DIN, name approval, and MOA/AOA drafting. Package prices vary based on authorized capital and additional services required.
